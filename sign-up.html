How to sign up

Registration for club membership and Athletic Union affiliation can be done online, via the Students Union website. Alternatively, you can pay in person at the Athletic Union offices in the Students Union building on Senghenydd Road, Cardiff.

You will need to pay the Cardiff University Athletic Union to affiliate with them. For Cardiff University students it costs £20 for the entire year. We don't control this fee and it is the same no matter which sports club you choose to join.

The 2020/21 fees for Cardiff University Aikido Club are as follows:

  • ​Aikido Club Annual membership - £45.00
  • British Aikido Board Membership - INCLUDED
  • Fudoshin Aikido Association Membership - FREE
  • Per Class Mat Fees - FREE
  • Grading Fees - FREE

Cardiff University Students can also train at our associate club, Fudoshin Aikido Cardiff, on Mondays, 19:00 - 21:00 at Chapter Arts Centre, for FREE, as part of your membership.

The only additional costs to students are to attend weekend seminars and courses, as detailed on our Events page. However, these are heavily subsidised by the Fudoshin Aikido Association, with discounted rates for students.

What you will receive

  • Up to 3 Aikido classes per week
  • High quality instruction from fully qualified teachers
  • British Aikido Board membership, insuring you to practice at any affiliated club or event
  • The opportunity to attend seminars featuring Internationally recognised teachers
  • Gradings, usually once per year
  • A Beginners Guide booklet
  • A Membership Book to record your grading progress & attendance at special events
  • Inclusive social events organised by the club committee throughout the year​

What if I don't study at Cardiff University?

While the club is run by and primarily for students at Cardiff University, it is still possible for us to accept other members. For example: If you are a member of staff at Cardiff University or a student at another university you can still join, but you will have to pay an additional affiliate fee to the Athletic Union.

It is best to speak to a committee officer before you sign up, as we have to inform the Athletic Union before any non Cardiff University students join the club.
