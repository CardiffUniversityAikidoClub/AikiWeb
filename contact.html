What to wear to your first class

If you are just starting out, any loose fitting, comfortable clothing will be fine. We recommend sweat pants and an old t-shirt or sweatshirt.

Once you are sure you would like to continue practicing Aikido, we can help source a keikogi (practice uniform) for you at massively discounted prices, so please ask one of our instructors if you are thinking about investing in one.
