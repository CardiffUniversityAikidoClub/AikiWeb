Welcome to our club

Cardiff University Aikido Club is a well-established and vibrant martial arts club, enjoying excellent relationships with other aikido clubs across the UK and internationally. We are affiliated to the British Aikido Board (UK Governing body) through our membership of the Fudoshin Aikido Association.

Aikido is a classical Japanese Martial Art that is non-competitive and suitable for all ages and abilities. It is a means of coordinating body and mind; a way of learning to relax; it is a road to becoming stronger, fitter and more flexible; it is non-aggressive and combines gentle, therapeutic exercise with a powerful system of self defence.

Training with us

Our classes take place at Talybont Sports Centre twice a week during term time:

  • Wednesdays: 20:30 - 22:00

  • Saturdays: 14:00 - 16:00

Please try to arrive at least 15 minutes early for each class, to allow time to change and make your way to the dojo area.

We welcome new members and visitors to our club throughout the academic year and whether you are a complete beginner or seasoned practitioner, your first class is always free.

Please feel free to explore our website, where you will find more information about our club, including upcoming events, how to find our dojo and how to sign up for Aikido as a student of Cardiff University.
