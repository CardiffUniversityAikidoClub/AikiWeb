Greg Habert (5th Dan Aikido and Iaido) Weekend Seminar

Fudoshin Aikido Cardiff are delighted to welcome Greg Habert sensei back to the UK this October for a 4 day weekend seminar. Greg belongs to a generation of new young teachers emerging in Europe. He is a superb technician and instructor, whose powerful, fluid Aikido has earned him international attention. Discounted admission for Cardiff University Aikido students!

Stephane Benedetti (7th Dan Aikido) Weekend Seminar

Stéphane Benedetti (7th Dan Shihan, Aikikai Hombu) returns to the UK for an open seminar. Stéphane is a truly inimitable presence in the dojo, and an illuminating teacher. His instruction focuses determinedly on the basics of Aikido, which is where he insists its secrets are to be found. Discounted admission for Cardiff University Aikido students!
