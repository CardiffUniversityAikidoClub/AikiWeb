Aikido 'Give it a Go' Session - No.1


Date:   Wednesday 06 October 2021

Time:   20:30 - 22:00

Place:   Talybont Sports Centre


Through participation in Cardiff University's 'Give It A Go' scheme, we are offering you the opportunity to try a traditional Japanese martial art for free!

You can participate in an introductory Aikido class, meet the instructors and current students, and learn some basic techniques - all in a safe, friendly and supportive environment.


COVID-19 Guidance

  • If you have symptoms or suspect that you have coronavirus then please do not attend the session and follow the NHS guidance to self isolate.

  • Please try to arrive at the dojo 15 minutes early, to allow us to complete the safety protocols we are obliged to follow.

  • There may be temperature checks on arrival and hand sanitiser will be provided, though you are free to use your own should you wish.

  • All participants will need to complete a form to “opt-in” to practice and provide contact details for track & trace purposes. This will apply to each individual for their first session only.

  • You are not obliged to wear a face covering during practice, but you may do so if you wish.

  • If you have any questions or concerns about Aikido training during COVID-19, please email us.
